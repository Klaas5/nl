Klaas De Baere

Performer, sculptural painter and visual theatre maker.

Starting from his illustrations, Klaas departs on a journey through his mental village.
Shapes that originate in his grandparents' village develop into large installations which he uses as decors in his narrative landscape.
Writing plays a big part in his art. He translates his texts into shapes, which join to become more than the sum of their parts.
In his decors, Klaas plays with a sense of depth, flatness, storytelling and shape.
